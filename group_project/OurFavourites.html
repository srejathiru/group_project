Taro Milk Tea

This is known to be the most popular in the store. So, what is all the hype about? Let’s start off by saying this tastes best with no ice and 100% sugar. So, coming to the taste, this extravagant creamy milk tea is like no other! Totally my favourite tea by far. Even people that are not a fan of milk tea would find this amazing! It doesn’t taste like pure milk tea and the tapioca pearls add a distinct taste that will make you addicted to this flavour! Another important addition; the tapioca pearls at T4 are by far the best we have ever tried; they are chewy and compliment the tea perfectly! This is known to be T4’s special, what are you waiting for?! Go ahead and try it!

Elegant Rose Royal tea with pearls

Although T4 is known best for its milk teas, the Elegant Rose Royal tea is nothing less exciting! T4 have an amazing way to make your drink according to your personal preferences by controlling the ice and sugar levels. This allows each flavour to be open to all customers. My preference is low level of ice and more sugar. Therefore, I get 120% sugar and 30% ice with this drink so that it keeps its flavour. The more ice you add the more the flavour is diluted so choose wisely kids! This flavour is perfect for those who don’t like milk tea but likes the flavour the tapioca pearls add. This also tastes amazing without the tapioca pearls, but it depends purely on personal preferences I would personally recommend the tea with the tapioca pearls. Go and try this amazing drink my lovelies!

Apple, White Peach, Lychee with Lychee Boba

This is the groups favourite at Bubbleology. We always somehow ended up at Bubbleology after lectures every Monday as a routine to try out a crazy combination of flavours each week. And to this day the amount of different flavours that are available till overwhelms me. The fusion of various different flavours which aren’t normally available in stores appeal to anyone looking for a refreshing beverage. Personally I (Vilisha) always go for this mix no matter what mood I’m in as it fits every occasion. Whether you want to celebrate after exams, take a break during shopping or just chill with friends it’s perfect.

Classic Rose milk tea with pearls

If you are a fan of plain milk tea and would like to add some flavour to experiment, you’re going to love this Classic Rose Milk Tea from T4! It tastes exactly like a classic milk tea with a twist and those who particular like a sweet flavour in their tea would love this! This tea would taste best with 30% ice and 120% sugar. This is just a recommendation though feel free to experiment with different sugar and ice levels too. It does make a noticeable difference to your drink. The tapioca pearls are the best topping with this drink and is definitely worth the extra 50p. What are you waiting for go and check it out!
