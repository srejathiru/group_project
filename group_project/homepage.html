Introduction

Here we will be sharing the different bubble tea flavours we have tried with you and give our top recommendations.

Bubble tea, also known as tapioca tea or milk tea, originates in Taiwan where you can find bubble tea stands on every corner.

The “bubbles” come from the froth that the milk makes when the drink is shaken prior to drinking.

The pearls in the bottom of the glass are tapioca pearls that are typically black in colour but can also be white or clear. They are soaked in a simple syrup before using so that they add sweetness to the drink.

After the pearls are placed in the glass, it is then filled with a chilled drink then shaken.
